The Rites of Passage, and the Apprenticeship of Essiahs

It was a Golden Age.

The Mighty Warrior-Poets of the Debh'Ops clan worked together with The Estiers and the Kuh'Undar clans and together there was peace, prosperity and harmony in the Land.

Essiahs had journeyed to the edge of the Land and trained at The Camp to hone his skills and to learn the Feats of the Warrior Poets and the Words of Power.

Essiahs journeyed far to the east, arriving at The Camp on the shores of the Land
He learnt to control his fears, and mastered the Wall of Fire
Essiahs became one of the Heroes of The Camp ...
and joined The Fellowship of the Wheel
Essiahs studied hard, long into the night ...
learning the secrets of the Shell and the Words of Power
